About Cakemade Bakery And Cafe

Born out of a passion to create that perfect café-styled environment where good coffee and sublime food are not merely enjoyed, but appreciated. Life's simple pleasures are celebrated in this warm and welcoming environment. Our bakery provides the ideal venue for new experiences and memories to be created. At our Lagos cafe, you may choose from a broad variety of freshly ground coffees, pastries, sweets, cold beverages, and superb café-themed meals to establish the atmosphere and tone for the discerning gourmet enthusiast.
